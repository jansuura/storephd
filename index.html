About Me

I am an Assistant Systems Analyst and Head,ICT Directorate , Navrongo Campus, of the University for Development Studies (UDS), Ghana and have been with UDS since September 2009. I am currently a PhD Candidate (Research Assistant) in computer science and Technology from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China . I received my Master Degree (2015), in Computer science and Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China Chengdu, China under the supervision of Prof. Xia Qi and Bachelor Degree (2007) in computer science from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi, Ghana.

Research Interest
